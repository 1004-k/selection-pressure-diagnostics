Figure 1. Pressure-indexed Type I error and power for standardized IPCW and DR-AIPW. Each point represents one deviation mechanism scenario. The x-axis is the median across replicates of the maximum absolute SPD(t) under the causal null. The top row shows Type I error under the null, and the bottom row shows power under the non-null setting. Panels compare richer and reduced baseline nuisance sets. The dashed line marks 0.05 in the Type I error panels.

Figure 2. Operating map over selection pressure and prognostic information quality. Each point is one scenario-regime combination, positioned by the SPD(t)-based pressure summary and measured prognostic information quality. The categories summarize whether DR-AIPW behaved as a reasonable default or required caution relative to standardized IPCW. Scenario IDs and full performance metrics are provided in the online tables.
